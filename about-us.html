About us

MineBest Sp. z o.o. is one of the most dynamically developing companies in the cryptocurrency mining industry. The core business entails building cryptocurrency mining farms and providing the service of professional hosting of mining equipment. Togather with our clients, MineBest is implementing a new standard for the mining ecosystem.

MineBest Sp. z o.o. is located in Warsaw (Poland) and was established in 2017 by a group of entrepreneurs engaged in the field of mining and cryptocurrencies. Our exeperience in the construction and maintenance of professional mining farms goes back to 2015.

The objective of MineBest is to provide the private consumer with the option of investing in the best and most advanced mining equipment of its kind in the world, and ensure secure facility, maintained 24/7 with the highest level of service in the field.

MineBest has a Representative Office in Israel as well as R&D and Sales Office in Poland. We will be happy to arrange a call and / or acquaintance meeting and provide the best service possible.
